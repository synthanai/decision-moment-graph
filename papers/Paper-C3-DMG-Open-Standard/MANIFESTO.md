# MANIFESTO: DMG Open Standard Paper

> **One-line:** Decisions need a data standard the way financial transactions have SWIFT. DMG is that standard.

---

## The Battle Cry

Financial transactions have SWIFT. Supply chains have GS1. Healthcare records have HL7. But organizational decisions have... nothing. No standard format for recording what was decided, why, by whom, with what dissent, under what conditions, and with what reversibility.

DMG (Decision Moment Graph) is an open data standard with 8 primitives (MEMO, MOMENT, RAMP, DOORS, DISSENT, TRACE, COMMIT, OUTCOME) that makes decisions as portable and auditable as financial transactions.

## What Gap This Fills

No open standard exists for decision provenance. DMG fills the same role for decisions that SWIFT fills for payments: a universal, interoperable format that makes organizational thinking machine-readable.

## The Intended Effect

> *"Why don't we have a SWIFT for decisions? Now we do."*
