# MANIFESTO: DMG Position Paper (F4)

> **One-line:** Open standards for AI decision governance. The case for DMG.

---

The EU AI Act demands decision auditability. Organizations are scrambling. But there is no open standard for decision provenance. DMG is a proposal for that standard: open, interoperable, auditable by design. This paper makes the public case.
