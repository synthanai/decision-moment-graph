# MANIFESTO: The DMS Octagon Paper

> **One-line:** Every decision your AI makes should pass through 8 gates. Here's the standard.

---

## The Battle Cry

AI agents today make decisions in a single step: prompt → response → action. No deliberation phase. No risk assessment phase. No reversibility check. No retrospective loop.

The Decision Moment Standard (DMS) Octagon is an 8-phase governance protocol: FRAME → SPAR → GATE → COMMIT → ENACT → YIELD → GAUGE → RETRO. Each phase is a gate that a decision must pass through, with the depth of scrutiny proportional to the decision's risk level (RAMP: trivial → significant → critical → existential).

The contribution: a formal decision governance standard that works for both human and AI decision-makers, with built-in reversibility, dissent preservation, and retrospective learning.

## What Gap This Fills

- **OODA Loop** (Boyd): 4 phases, no governance gates, no risk calibration
- **PDCA** (Deming): 4 phases, quality-focused, no deliberation or dissent
- **Design Thinking**: 5 phases, creativity-focused, no decision auditability
- **DMS Octagon**: 8 phases, governance-focused, risk-calibrated, AI-compatible

## The Intended Effect

> *"This is the ISO 9001 for decisions. Not for products, for the thinking behind products."*
